Idea #1

For football fans who enjoy the strategy and chess match of the sport, Gridiron Tactics is a turn based football game that puts the player in the coach's role of the play caller and decision maker at each turn.
Unlike other football games that have you directly control players in a more arcade or unrealistic fashion, our game will give you important team management details and tactical options that help determine the outcome of each game using realistic player ratings and statistics.

Idea #2

For casual gamers who enjoy a fun strategy game, who might also get excited about creating prehistoric match-ups, Dino Tactics is a turn based battle game that will present you with strategic choices as you battle against different prehistoric beasts.
Unlike other other strategy games that may bog you down with microtransactions or a confusing amount of depth, Dino Tactics will strive for fun and quick gameplay with just enough depth to keep you coming back into the awesome and unlimited setting of prehistoric times.

Idea #3

For card and collectible enthusiasts who want to be able to manage and keep track of their inventory, the Fan Vault is an inventory tracking and database application that keeps track of your collectibles while easily presenting you with valuable information and statistics.
Unlike regular homemade spreadsheets, lists, or document files, our product will bring you the information you want about your collectibles in an easy to use fashion while also giving you a showcase to visually see and share your prized possessions.
